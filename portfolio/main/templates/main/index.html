ABOUT

Me

Introduction

Welcome, I am Miklos Rothweil your host.

I am a mechatronics engineer, with automation and maintenance engineering experience. Both of these fields demanded me to get in touch with the programing side of my profession.

In my spare time, besides doing sports, or watching movies, I do arduino projects where my love for electronics and programming unite.

Motivation

My motivation to learn and start a career in back-end development comes from multiple sources.

Working with automated production lines/machines I had to learn their programing languages (PLCs, robots) in order to solve unwanted or unexpected malfunctions. And besides coffee, nothing gives me a bigger push to keep on learning then a successful problem-solving. Maybe just the unsuccessful ones...

In my case, university gave me a little hands-on experience to handle my work tasks, so I had to get involved with self-teaching. Thanks to it, at the peak of my engineering career I became a senior engineer, getting the most challenging tasks and being able to teach my colleagues. The experience of self-teaching and the accomplishment which came after gives me the courage to knock on the door of this new field.

At last, if one would ask, why back-end? During my research of the different fields of the IT sector and the satisfaction that came from deploying my first webpage thanks to an excellent udemy course, brought back-end development closes to my heart.

I am looking for a junior position in order to jump-start my Developer career and learn from the experienced!
